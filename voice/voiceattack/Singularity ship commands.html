Elite: Singularity voice commands    
Words in [XXX] are dynamic commands. So [say hello to the stream;introduce yourself]  can be said as 'say hello to the stream or 'introduce yourself and the command will be executed. The semicolon on at the end of a selection makes the section optional, so [all stations;] [say hello to the stream;introduce yourself] lets you say the following to execute the command: "all stations say hello to the stream" or "all stations introduce yourself" or  "say hello to the stream" or "introduce yourself".
Some commands may have several sections ie [xxx;yyy] [xxx;yyy;] [xxx;yyy;zzz]
[hi;hello;greetings] [crewman;crewmen] [alix;archer;astra;celeste;cleo;dark;doris;eden;eli;jazz;kate;k s a i;k s i;legend;legion;leo;mars;midnight;minus;obsidian;orion;phoenix;vasco;vega;venus;verity;ze]
"Hi crewman Alix" or "Hello crewman Leo" or "Greetings crewman Vasco"
Please note: Some words are the phonetic spelling and not the correct spelling - so it's not a bug :) Description Category
